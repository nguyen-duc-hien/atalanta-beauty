How to enroll

Our streamlined process makes it easy to find the perfect candidate for your needs, saving you time and money.

Schedule now! School catalog
Step 1

Get in touch

Fill out the request for information form on our website, give us a call or text.

Step 2

Schedule a Tour

You will get a behind-the-scenes look at the campus during the tour. You will meet instructors, observe students in action,...

Step 3

Fill Out Your Application

Your admission coordinator will help you walk through the application process and answer any question you may have.

Step 4

Sign Your Contract

You will meet with your admissions coordinator to go over funding and choose your start date. A $25.00 fee is due at the time of signing and than you will be set!

What to brings

While it isn’t required you bring anything, we do suggest you bring a few things:

  • A List of Questions - We want you to get the most out of your time with us and coming with a list of questions can help!
  • A Pen & Paper - You most likely will not remember everything during our tour, so this is a great way to document the necessary information.
  • A Friend or Family Member - We all need a good support system while making a big decision!
